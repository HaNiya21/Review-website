The Purpose of this Website

This website aim is to give a genuine review of books, movies, TV shows, local restaurants, recreation places, music albums, shopping places, and websites. Of course, reviews for such things can be found on yelp, maps, IMDB, Amazon, Google, etc. However, this site won't work to benefit or hurt any of the people and their reputations that due to the review have been going to be given. Therefore, people come to this website to give their genuine opinion on anything they feel like sharing, good or bad.
